Le Demain

Le Demain is a non-profit project which is operated by students from different areas of Vietnam. With our enthusiasm and passion, we hope to be able to impart information about common operating modes of projects, essential factors, as well as common issues when engaging in extracurricular activities.

About us

2020

The first season called “Périple - Journey” achieved certain successes with the completion exceeding some targets such as attaining more than 4.1 thousand followers and likes on Facebook, receiving positive feedback from not only students but also parents and people who are interested in extracurricular activities. Through the experiences of the participants in the first season event, Le Demain has spread the significance of extracurricular activities to the community. Besides, our project has contributed small but extremely meaningful values to society by helping students to gain access to extracurricular activities, acquire the necessary skills and confidence to become a better version of themselves.
